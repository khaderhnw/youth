S3 • Decide & Plan
S3-A • Choose Path Scenario
Cross-journey
17–21
Entities: ADEK, ACTVET, NSRA, MAWAHEB
  • “Plan my path” wizard offers scenarios: study-first, NS-first, work-first, mixed.

S3 • Decide & Plan
S3-B • Link Goals to Programmes
Cross-journey
18–23
Entities: ADEK, ACTVET, NSRA, MAWAHEB, VOLAE, Ma’an, Shory, KFED
  • For each Vision Board goal, youth picks 1–3 concrete initiatives (scholarship, NS track, job programme, volunteering, Ghaya, Shory, KFED, etc.).
  • Board shows a matrix of goals vs. attached initiatives.
  • Gaps (goals with no actions) are highlighted to youth & dashboards.

Cross-journey
18–23
Entities: ADEK, ACTVET, NSRA, MAWAHEB, VOLAE, Ma’an, Shory, KFED, YES2WORK, Youth Platform

S5 • Participate & Track
S5-A • One View of Progress
Cross-journey
18–23
Entities: All programme owners surfaced through Youth Platform
  • “My Journey” shows tiles for NS, education, work, volunteering, Ghaya, Shory policies, entrepreneurship, wellbeing.
