Coming of Age – Unified To‑Be Journey on TAMM Youth

This page shows the unified youth life‑moment journey across education, volunteering, National Service, first job, entrepreneurship and wellbeing – all delivered through TAMM Youth. Each row is a key touchpoint in the journey.

Stage 1 – Onboard to TAMM Youth

ID Subjourney Persona Youth touchpoint TAMM frontstage Entities / systems
1.1 Platform onboarding P1–P3 Hears that TAMM Youth is the one place for education, National Service, volunteering, jobs and entrepreneurship. Shows a Youth landing page with a simple narrative, explainer video, and tiles for key areas (Education, NS, Volunteering, First Job, Entrepreneurship, Wellbeing) plus a “Start your journey” button. TAMM, FYA, ADEK, ACTVET, NSRA, KFED, volunteers.ae, AD Police, Ma’an, FCA, ADS/Nafis.
1.2 Platform onboarding P1–P3 Logs in with UAE Pass / Emirates ID and expects personalised content. Performs SSO with UAE Pass, reads age, gender and nationality, sets youth segment and language, and shows relevant tiles (e.g. “Grade 11 student”, “NS-eligible soon”). TAMM, UAE Pass, population registry.
1.3 Data & consent P1–P3 Wants to share data once so they don’t keep re-entering education, volunteering, NS, job and business details. Displays a single consent hub with toggles to connect ADEK, ACTVET, volunteers.ae, NSRA, ADS/Nafis, KFED, AD Police, FCA and Ma’an; clearly explains what is used and why. ADEK, ACTVET, volunteers.ae, NSRA, ADS/Nafis, KFED, AD Police, FCA, Ma’an.
1.4 Youth 360 profile P1–P3 Fills in interests, favourite subjects, work preferences, volunteering themes and entrepreneurship interest. Runs a step‑by‑step Youth 360 wizard with an optional interest / personality quiz; only asks for data not available from connected systems. FYA, ADEK, ACTVET, employers, KFED.
1.5 Youth dashboard P1–P3 Wants a quick overview of where they are and what they can do next. Presents a Youth Dashboard with tiles for Education & Scholarships, Volunteering & Hours, National Service, First Job & Internships, Entrepreneurship, and Wellbeing & Safety, each with status. All integrated entities via TAMM.

Stage 2 – Discover & Explore Opportunities

ID Subjourney Persona Youth touchpoint TAMM frontstage Entities / systems
2.1 Education & scholarships P1–P2 Clicks Education & Scholarships and wants to see all options in one list. Shows scholarships and programmes with filters for major, location, GPA, NS timing and EmSAT; clearly labels degree vs TVET vs foundation. ADEK, ADS scholarships, universities, ACTVET.
2.2 TVET & skills P1–P2 Looks for vocational and skills‑based paths instead of only traditional degrees. Displays TVET and skills catalogue (ACTVET, ADVETI, accredited providers, Emirates Skills) with entry requirements and pathways from grade 12. ACTVET, ADVETI, ADEK, private training centres.
2.3 Volunteering & community P1–P3 Searches for volunteering opportunities to reach required hours and build a CV. Provides a volunteering explorer fed from volunteers.ae and partners, with filters for location, cause, skills and format; shows current hours total and target. volunteers.ae, ADEK, Ma’an, NGOs, school systems.
2.4 National Service awareness P1–P2 Wants to understand what National Service is, when it applies, and how it affects studies and work. Shows an NSRA information card with personalised call‑up window, service type options, simple timeline and FAQs. NSRA, MOD, ADEK, employers.
2.5 First job & work experience P1–P3 Looks for safe first work experiences (Yes to Work, internships, part‑time jobs). Lists youth‑friendly programmes (Yes to Work, internships, part‑time roles) with age, eligibility and NS implications labelled. ACTVET, ADS job portals, Nafis/Tawteen, employers.
2.6 Entrepreneurship & wellbeing P2–P4 Is curious about entrepreneurship and wants life skills and safety guidance. Shows an Entrepreneurship hub with KFED programmes and an “Am I ready?” quiz, plus a Wellbeing & Safety hub (financial literacy, cyber safety, anti‑drugs, helplines). KFED, FCA, AD Police, Ma’an, FYA.

Stage 3 – Decide & Apply

ID Subjourney Persona Youth touchpoint TAMM frontstage Entities / systems
3.1 Education & scholarships P1–P2 Shortlists a few universities or scholarships and needs help comparing. Provides a compare view (GPA, EmSAT, cost, location, NS timing, job prospects) and a button to talk to a counsellor. ADEK, ADS scholarships, universities, ACTVET, schools.
3.2 Education & scholarships P1–P2 Books a session with a counsellor to choose major or path. Enables booking of school counsellor / career advisor sessions (virtual or in‑person) and shares the Youth 360 profile. ADEK, schools, ACTVET, FYA.
3.3 Volunteering P1–P3 Chooses a volunteering opportunity and submits an application, sometimes needing parent consent. Uses a unified application form with Emirates ID / UAE Pass and digital parent consent, showing expected hours and how they will be recognised. volunteers.ae, ADEK, Ma’an, event organisers.
3.4 National Service P2–P3 Prepares to register for NS and wants to avoid multiple centre visits. Embeds NSRA pre‑registration with a clear doc checklist, upload capability and booking for NS centre and medical tests. NSRA, NS medical clinics, population registry.
3.5 First job & work experience P2–P3 Needs a strong CV and wants to apply for internships or first jobs. Offers a CV and profile builder that pulls education, volunteering, NS status and certificates, with one‑click apply to jobs on ADS/Nafis/Tawteen. ADS job portals, Nafis/Tawteen, employers, ACTVET, volunteers.ae.
3.6 Entrepreneurship P2–P4 Has a business idea and wants to submit it to KFED and speak to someone. Provides a simple KFED idea wizard and appointment booking with a KFED advisor. KFED, banks, incubators.
3.7 Wellbeing & safety P1–P3 Feels stressed or at risk and looks for confidential support. Shows a discreet “Need help?” entry to FCA, Ma’an and health support, plus registration for AD Police and FCA awareness programmes. FCA, Ma’an, AD Police, health / mental health partners.
3.8 Cross‑journey documents P1–P3 Is confused by different documentation requirements across scholarships, NS, jobs and KFED. Provides a smart checklist that aggregates document requirements, shows what’s already on file and what’s missing, and allows direct upload. ADEK, NSRA, ADS/Nafis, KFED, volunteers.ae.

Stage 4 – Participate & Track

ID Subjourney Persona Youth touchpoint TAMM frontstage Entities / systems
4.1 Education & scholarships P1–P2 Wants to see scholarship and admission status without calling different entities. Displays a consolidated “Track my education applications” view with statuses, missing documents and deadlines. ADEK, ADS scholarships, universities, ACTVET.
4.2 Volunteering P1–P3 Attends volunteering events and needs proof of hours. Offers QR / OTP check‑in and automatically updates volunteering hours and certificates in the Youth Record. volunteers.ae, event organisers, ADEK, NSRA, Ma’an.
4.3 National Service P2–P3 During NS, wants to know where they are in the process and what comes next. Shows an NS tracker card with current phase, benefits and a countdown to completion, plus links to allowed TAMM services. NSRA, MOD, partner universities and employers.
4.4 First job & work experience P2–P3 Has multiple job applications and wants to track them in one place. Provides a pipeline of applications with statuses and required actions (interview, medical, contract signing, onboarding tasks). ADS job portals, Nafis/Tawteen, employers, health checks, insurance providers.
4.5 Entrepreneurship P2–P4 Joined KFED training or loan process and wants to track progress. Shows stages such as pre‑screening, training, feasibility, committee, disbursement and mentoring, with due dates and tasks. KFED, banks, training partners.
4.6 Wellbeing & safety P1–P3 Attends safety, life skills or prevention sessions and wants recognition. Provides a calendar of sessions (road safety, cyber safety, anti‑drugs, financial literacy), handles registration and adds digital badges to the Youth Record. AD Police, FCA, Ma’an, schools, health partners.
4.7 Cross‑journey visibility P1–P4 Wants to see all education, NS, volunteering, job and entrepreneurship activities together. Shows a “My Journey” timeline combining milestones and highlighting completed, active and upcoming items. All integrated entities via TAMM.
4.8 Cross‑journey nudges P1–P3 Needs reminders to avoid missing important deadlines or appointments. Runs a smart notification engine that sends reminders for deadlines and allows quick actions (reschedule, upload, confirm). TAMM notifications, ADEK, NSRA, volunteers.ae, ADS/Nafis, KFED, AD Police, FCA.

Stage 5 – Transition & Next Steps

ID Subjourney Persona Youth touchpoint TAMM frontstage Entities / systems
5.1 School graduation → next P1 Finishes grade 12 and is unsure what to do next. Detects graduation and triggers a “What’s next?” wizard suggesting NS, higher education, TVET, Yes to Work, volunteering and entrepreneurship based on their profile. ADEK, NSRA, ACTVET, ADS, volunteers.ae, KFED.
5.2 NS completion → next P2–P3 Completes National Service and needs to transition to studies or work. Uses NS completion to unlock tiles for jobs, education and entrepreneurship, and highlights NS‑friendly roles. NSRA, ADEK, ADS/Nafis, KFED, volunteers.ae.
5.3 Higher education completion → job P2–P3 Graduates from university/TVET and wants a first long‑term job. Detects graduation and recommends job matches, alumni networks and upskilling, converting Youth Record into a professional profile. Universities, ACTVET, ADS/Nafis, employers.
5.4 Job search → alternative paths P2–P3 Has been searching for jobs for a long time and feels stuck. After repeated rejections, suggests alternative paths such as reskilling, apprenticeships and entrepreneurship via KFED. ADS/Nafis, ACTVET, KFED, FYA.
5.5 Volunteering → leadership P1–P3 Has high volunteering hours and wants to take on leadership roles. Recognises high contribution and proposes roles as youth leader, mentor, or member of youth councils and national campaigns. volunteers.ae, FYA, schools, Ma’an.
5.6 Risk & support P1–P3 Shows patterns such as NS dropout or repeated job rejections and may need support. Offers wellbeing check‑ins and directs youth to support programmes rather than penalties, with clear privacy safeguards. FCA, Ma’an, AD Police (awareness), health and mental health partners, schools.
5.7 Youth Record → external use P2–P4 Needs to show history to employers, scholarship committees or visa processes. Generates a sharable digital Youth Record summarising education, NS, volunteering, work and entrepreneurship achievements. All data‑providing entities; visa and employer systems.

Stage 6 – Ongoing Support & Engagement

ID Subjourney Persona Youth touchpoint TAMM frontstage Entities / systems
6.1 Continuous learning P2–P4 Already working or studying and wants to keep learning. Recommends relevant micro‑credentials, online courses and competitions, all linked back to their Youth Record. ACTVET, universities, competition organisers, EdTech partners.
6.2 Volunteering & community P1–P4 Wants to stay active in community service. Suggests long‑term volunteering roles, committees and community projects according to history and interests. volunteers.ae, Ma’an, NGOs, FYA.
6.3 NS & youth alumni networks P2–P4 Wants to remain involved after NS or youth programmes. Connects them to alumni networks, NS youth councils and national initiatives under a dedicated “Communities” section. NSRA, FYA, Emirates Youth Council, Ma’an, employers.
6.4 Career progression P2–P4 Has a job but wants to progress or change sector. Provides career pathway suggestions, mentoring and targeted opportunities through Nafis/Tawteen and employer programmes. ADS/Nafis, employers, ACTVET, universities, FYA.
6.5 Entrepreneurship growth P3–P4 Has started a business and wants to grow or expand. Shows growth‑stage services such as additional KFED support, export programmes and hiring incentives. KFED, economic departments, banks, chambers of commerce.
6.6 Ongoing wellbeing & safety P1–P4 Needs ongoing support to stay safe, resilient and financially literate. Runs continuous campaigns in the Youth feed around safety, mental health and financial literacy, triggered by key life moments. AD Police, FCA, Ma’an, health / mental health partners, FYA.
